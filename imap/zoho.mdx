---

Zoho Mail is popular for businesses, but it strictly separates "User Passwords" from "App Passwords."

## 1. Enable IMAP Access

1.  Log in to Zoho Mail.
2.  Click the **Gear Icon** (Settings).
3.  Go to **Mail Accounts** and find the **IMAP** section.
4.  Ensure the **IMAP Access** checkbox is **Checked**.

## 2. Get App Password (Required if TFA is On)

If you use Two-Factor Authentication (TFA) to log in to Zoho, Flowiz cannot use your normal password.

1.  Click your profile picture -> **My Account**.
2.  Select **Security** in the left menu.
3.  Click **App Passwords** -> **Generate New Password**.
4.  **App Name:** Enter `Flowiz` and copy the code.
