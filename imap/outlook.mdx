Microsoft blocks external tools by default. You must enable "Syncing" to let Flowiz read your replies.

## Enable POP/IMAP

1.  Log in to Outlook.com or Office 365.
2.  Click the **Gear Icon** (Settings) -> **View all Outlook settings** (link at bottom).
3.  Navigate to **Mail** -> **Sync email**.
4.  Scroll down to the **POP and IMAP** section.

### Required Settings:
* **Let devices and apps use POP:** Select **Yes**.
* **Let devices and apps use IMAP:** Select **Yes**.

Click **Save**. You can now use your standard Outlook email and password inside Flowiz.
