The **WhatsApp Agent** uses the official **Meta Cloud API**. This ensures high deliverability and protects your number from getting banned.

## 1. Connecting to Meta

To enable WhatsApp, you must authorize Flowiz to access your Meta Business Manager.

**Step 1:** Go to the **Agents** tab and click the **Settings (Gear)** icon next to WhatsApp.

**Step 2:** Click the blue **Connect Meta** button.

**Step 3:** You will be redirected to the **Templates** page.

### Authorizing the App

On the Templates page, click the **Connect Meta** button (top right). A popup will appear asking you to log in to Facebook and select your Business Account.

<img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/whatsapp-templates-connect-light.png" alt="Templates Connect Light" />

## 2. Creating Message Templates

WhatsApp requires all initial messages to be pre-approved "Templates". You cannot send random text to a new lead.

**1. Add Template:** Click the pink **+ Add New Template** button.

**2. Open Form:** A form will slide out from the right side.

**3. Fill Details:**
* **Category:** Choose "Marketing" or "Utility".
* **Body:** Write your message content (e.g., `Hi {{name}}...`).
* **Buttons:** Add a "Quick Reply" or "Call to Action".

**4. Submit:** Click **Submit**.

<img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/whatsapp-create-form-light.png" alt="Create Template Form Light" />
<img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/whatsapp-create-form-dark.png" alt="Create Template Form Dark" />

## 3. Approval & Testing

Once submitted, Meta will review your template (usually within minutes).

Check the **Status** column in the Templates table:
* **PENDING:** Meta is reviewing it.
* **APPROVED:** You can now use this template.
* **REJECTED:** Check the error and try again.
