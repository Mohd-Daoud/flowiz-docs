The **WhatsApp Agent** uses the official **Meta Cloud API**. This ensures high deliverability and protects your number from getting banned.

## 1. Connecting to Meta

To enable WhatsApp, you must authorize Flowiz to access your Meta Business Manager.

1.  Go to the **Agents** tab and click the **Settings (Gear)** icon next to WhatsApp.
2.  Click the blue **Connect Meta** button.
3.  You will be redirected to the **Templates** page.

## 2. Creating Message Templates

WhatsApp requires all initial messages to be pre-approved "Templates". You cannot send random text to a new lead.

1.  Click the pink **+ Add New Template** button.
2.  A form will slide out from the right side.
3.  **Fill in the details:**
    * **Category:** Choose "Marketing" (for offers) or "Utility" (for updates).
    * **Body:** Write your message content (e.g., *"Hi {{name}}, here is your offer..."*).
    * **Buttons:** Add a "Quick Reply" or "Call to Action" button.
4.  Click **Submit**.

## 3. Approval & Testing

Once submitted, Meta will review your template (usually within minutes).

Check the **Status** column in the Templates table:
* **PENDING:** Meta is reviewing it.
* **APPROVED:** You can now use this template in your Automations.
* **REJECTED:** Check the error and try again.
