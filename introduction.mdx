## What is Flowiz?

Flowiz is a full automation ecosystem that uses intelligent **AI Agents** to replace manual sales work. Flowiz unifies the entire workflow into a single visual interface, in contrast to traditional tools that require you to connect disparate apps (such as scrapers, email verification, and CRMs).

## How It Works

Flowiz uses a straightforward 4-step logic to function. To create intricate automations, you don't have to be a developer.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Recipe or Write Prompt">
    Flowiz uses a straightforward 4-step logic to function. To create intricate automations, you don't have to be a developer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Your Leads">
    To find new prospects automatically, use the **Google Maps Extractor** or upload your customer list in **CSV/Excel**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate Agents">
    To allow the system to send messages on your behalf, connect your **Email (SMTP)** or **WhatsApp (Meta API)**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Campaign">
    Press **Start**. The AI takes over, interacting with your leads and monitoring responses in the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
