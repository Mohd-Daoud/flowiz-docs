Are you prepared to automate sales? Start your first "Cold Outreach" flow by following these four steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Automation">
    Go to the Automations tab. Click **+ Add Automation** and choose a “Cold Email” recipe, or write a prompt to create it from scratch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import Leads">
    Go to the **Leads** tab. Import your **CSV/Excel file** file with the names and emails of your prospects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Channels">
    Go to **Agents**. Click the gear icon next to **Email** and enter your SMTP details to authorize sending.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Campaign">
    Return to your Automation, select your lead list, and click **Start Campaign**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
