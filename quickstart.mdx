Ready to automate your sales? Follow this 3-step guide to launch your first "Cold Outreach" flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Email">
    Go to the **Agents** tab and click the gear icon next to **Email**. Enter your SMTP details (Gmail/Outlook) to enable sending.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Recipe">
    Go to **Automations** -> **+ Add Automation**. Choose **"Send personalized cold emails from Excel uploads"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload & Launch">
    Upload your CSV file of leads. The AI will automatically write the emails for you. Click **Start Campaign**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
