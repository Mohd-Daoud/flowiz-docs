---
title: Quickstart
description: "Launch your first automated campaign in 4 simple steps."
---

Follow this guide to set up your first outreach flow using the Flowiz dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Your Prompt">
    Log in to the dashboard. In the **"Prompt"** section, describe your business, target audience, and goal.
    
    *Example:* "I am a coffee wholesaler looking for cafes in New York to sell my beans to."
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Extract Leads">
    Choose your data source. You can:
    * **Extract:** Select "Google Maps" or "LinkedIn" to find new leads automatically.
    * **Upload:** Upload your own Excel or CSV file.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Agent">
    Toggle the agents you want to use for this campaign:
    * **Email Agent:** For formal introductions.
    * **WhatsApp Agent:** For quick, high-engagement messages.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Launch & Track">
    Click **"Start Flow"**. Your agents will begin working immediately. Monitor the **Analytics Dashboard** to see open rates and replies in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
